About Me

Hi! Welcome to my website :). I am Saurabh Bedi, a PhD student in Neuroeconomics at the Marlene Porsche Graduate School of Neuroeconomics.

My research interests broadly revolve around how we can understand the biases and variability in basic learning and decision making from the perspective of a noisy brain carrying out its computations under limited resource constraints.
